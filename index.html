¡Hola Mojácar!
Your guide to getting started
☀️
Hoy — Daily Cards
This is your main practice screen. Each day you get a fresh batch of flashcards based on your daily goal. Work through them one at a time — there's no rush.
👆
Using the Flashcards
Tap a card to reveal the translation. Try to recall the meaning before you tap — that's where the learning happens. Use the 🔊 button to hear the Spanish pronunciation.
✓
Got it ✓ — Be Honest!
Only tap Got it ✓ if you genuinely recalled or translated the phrase correctly. The app tracks your progress based on honest self-assessment. Tapping it when you weren't sure only cheats yourself!
🔁
Didn't Get It
Tap Didn't get it to send the card to the back of today's queue. You'll see it again before the session ends. No shame — repetition is how it sticks.
🏆
Mastery — 10 Recalls
A phrase is only marked as Mastered after 10 successful recalls across multiple sessions. Research shows this is the minimum needed for long-term retention. Keep going!
✏️
Typing Mode
Enable Typing mode in Settings to type out the translation before it's revealed. Your answer is checked automatically — green words matched, red words didn't. You can always override with Got it ✓.
🔄
English → Spanish Mode
Switch on English → Spanish in Settings for a harder challenge — you see the English and have to produce the Spanish. The audio is hidden until after you've answered. Accents won't count against you.
📚
Browse
Tap Browse to search all 456 phrases or filter by category — beach, bar, restaurant, gym and more. Great for looking something up before you head out.
📈
Progress
Track your daily streak, total phrases mastered, and review history. The chart shows your last 7 days or 4 weeks at a glance. Try to keep that streak alive!
⚙️
Settings
Adjust your daily card goal (5–50), toggle auto-play, enable typing mode or English→Spanish mode, and add the app to your iPhone home screen for a native app feel.
💡 Top tip: Even just 10 cards a day adds up fast. Consistency beats cramming every time — a 5-minute habit is worth more than an hour once a week.
